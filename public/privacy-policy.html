Overview

This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage or remove your information. Short version: we keep things minimal and useful.

Data we collect

Personal info

Name, email address, billing details you provide when you sign up or make a purchase.

  • • Name & contact
  • • Payment & billing
  • • Profile settings

Usage data

Non-identifying data about how you use the site — pages visited, features used, and technical info (browser, device, IP).

  • • Analytics (aggregated)
  • • Crash and performance logs
  • • Cookies & local storage

Cookies & tracking

We use cookies for essential site functionality and to improve the experience. You can control cookie preferences in your browser; essential cookies cannot be turned off because they power basic features.

How we use data

Provide & improve services

To deliver the features you use, process payments, and improve the product with aggregated analytics.

Communication

To send account-related notices, billing receipts, and updates you opt into.

Legal & safety

To protect our rights, comply with the law, and prevent abuse.

Sharing & third parties

We never sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers — payment processors, analytics providers, hosting; they process data on our behalf under contract.
  • Legal requests — if required by law, we may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

Access

Request a copy of your personal data.

Rectify

Update or correct inaccurate information.

Delete

Ask us to remove your data (subject to legal retention requirements).

To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below. We respond within a reasonable timeframe and may ask for identity verification to protect your privacy.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards such as encryption in transit (TLS), secure backups, and limited access controls. No system is 100% secure — we continuously monitor and improve.

Breach notification:

If a breach affects your personal data, we'll notify affected individuals and regulators as required by law.
