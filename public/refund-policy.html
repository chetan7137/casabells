Refunds available within 7–14 days depending on payment method, subject to eligibility and conditions outlined below.

Overview

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. This refund policy explains when and how refunds are available, along with the steps you can take to request one.

Eligibility

  • • Refunds apply to purchases made directly through our website.
  • • Digital products/services may only be refunded if unused or not yet accessed.
  • • Physical products must be returned in original condition within 7 days of delivery.

Refund timeline

Credit/Debit Card

5–7 business days

UPI / Wallet

3–5 business days

Bank Transfer

7–14 business days

Exceptions

Some items and services may not qualify for refunds, including but not limited to:

  • Customized or personalized items
  • Services already rendered
  • Discounted or clearance products (unless defective)

Refund process

Step 1: Request

Contact our support team with your order ID and reason for refund.

Step 2: Review

We will verify your request against our policy and may ask for additional details.

Step 3: Refund

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within the stated timelines.
